About ICCT-SD 2026

Bridging the gap between technological innovation and sustainable development through collaborative research and knowledge sharing.

Conference Vision

ICCT-SD 2026 aims to create a platform where researchers, academicians, and industry experts converge to discuss the latest developments in computational intelligence and communication technologies that contribute to sustainable development goals.

Our conference focuses on fostering innovation that addresses real-world challenges while promoting environmental sustainability and social responsibility.
